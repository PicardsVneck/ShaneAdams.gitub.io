Background

Hello! My name is Shane Adams and I have been a software engineer for 12+ years. I'm currently based in Austin Texas. I got my start working in the enterprise / security space for TxDOT, IBM, and as a software security engineer consultant for fortune 500 companies. I decided to make a career change and pursue my passion in the game development space. I worked as an independent developer to get some experience, then worked at SKillz as a Senior Unity SDK Engineer for a competitive multiplayer gaming platform. Nowdays I am traveling and working on passion projects.

Application and Game development is my true passion. I love creating interactive experiences for people to enjoy. I take a lot of joy in being able to work with a variety of different technologies and solving complex problems. I am always looking for opportunites to learn new things and grow as a developer.

When not working, I'm usually either working on a side project, traveling around the world, gaming with friends, weight training, or rock climbing.
